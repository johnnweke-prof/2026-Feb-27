# Agenda

---
- 6:30pm - 6:50pm  Meet and Greet _(upbeat christian music playing in the background)_

- 6:50pm - 7:00pm  **Icebreaker** (group icebreaker to get everyone moving. Suggestions welcome)

- 7:00pm - 7:05pm  Opening Prayer

- 7:05pm - 7:40pm  **Breakout Sessions:** Bible Study
      - Led by the Class Teachers and Assistants
      - Start with another small icebreaker to get to know the people (5 mins)
      - Do the study.
      - Remember to ask questions, get their engagement.
      - Ask the participants to read scripture for you, while everyone else looks into their bible.
      - Invite them to ask questions, or share what they got from it.
            - *(If you don't know the answer, thats OK - when we do the group session, we will answer the question)*
  
- 7:40pm - 7:45pm - Get everyone back together for a group session

- 7:45pm - 8:00pm - **Group Session**
      - 7:45pm - 7:50pm - Introduce the Tri-Boards (or Sign Up Sheets)
      - 7:50pm - 7:55pm - Ask about their experience, see if anyone wants to share what they learned.
      - 7:55pm - 8:00pm - Share 1-2 stories from current Victory Collective members (2-3 mins each)

- 8:00pm - 8:05pm - Altar Call/Prayer of Salvation

- 8:05pm - 8:35pm - Worship & Prayer

- 8:45pm - 9:15pm - Food and Fellowship _(Upbeat Christian music playing in the background)_

- 9:00pm - 9:30pm - Clean Up and pack up (Victory Collective Members)
